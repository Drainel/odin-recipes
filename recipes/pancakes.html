Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

A thin, flat, round cake made from flour, sugar, and eggs, cooked in a pan and usually eaten with maple syrup for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-prupose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Scoop the batter onto the surface, using around 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  4. Cook until brown on both sides and Voila!
